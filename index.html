About Us

Precision Tooling Excellence

Shanvi Dies and Moulds, founded on 1st September 2023, is a leading independent precision tooling provider with 18 years of expertise in high-quality dies, moulds, mould bases, automotive spare parts, and CNC machining. ISO 9001:2015 certified, we are dedicated to global quality standards, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement.

To provide ISO-standard products that meet global benchmarks. To deliver customized solutions tailored to client requirements. To build long-term partnerships through trust, commitment, and timely service.
